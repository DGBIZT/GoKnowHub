Основные эндпоинты для тестирования:

Курсы:

GET    http://127.0.0.1:8000/courses/
POST   http://127.0.0.1:8000/courses/
GET    http://127.0.0.1:8000/courses/<id>/
PUT    http://127.0.0.1:8000/courses/<id>/
DELETE http://127.0.0.1:8000/courses/<id>/

POST   http://127.0.0.1:8000/lesson/create/
GET    http://127.0.0.1:8000/lesson/
GET    http://127.0.0.1:8000/lesson/<id>/
PUT    http://127.0.0.1:8000/lesson/update/<id>/
DELETE http://127.0.0.1:8000/lesson/delete/<id>/
